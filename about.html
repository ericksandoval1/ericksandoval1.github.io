About the Artist

My name is Erick Sandoval. I was born and raised in Santa Ana, California. My love for art started at a very young age. When I was in elementary school I also went to a weekend tutoring program and that is where I first tried my hand in making art. From there I would do painting and drawing at home and school. It wasn't until highschool and college when I took digial art classes that I fell in love with graphic design and video editing.

My aspiration is to pursue a career in graphic design. I enjoy making artworks that involve editing, combining, and manipulating images. More recently I introduced video editing into my graphic design work which I am passionate about. I want to help create visually captivating and innovative designs that leave a lasting impression on the viewer.
