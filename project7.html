AI Comic Art, 2023

ChatGPT, Midjourney, and Photoshop

Description: This project was made using concepts generated from ChatGPT. Then in Midjourney, I wrote in prompt that would produce images relating to the concept. Finally, I used Photoshop to bring these elements together into a comic book format. This project is meant to showcase the way AI can be used as tool to graphic designers and also highlight the shortcomings of AI.
