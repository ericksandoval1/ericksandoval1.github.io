Artist Statement

I manipulate found videos and images together with my own artwork to make bright and nostalgic pieces. Through exploring the concept of nostalgia, I draw inspiration from the media of my early childhood to create art that connects the viewer to a positive thought or emotion.

I was born and raised in Santa Ana, CA. I am currently attending San Jose State University majoring in Digital Media Art.

Saturday Morning

This is a compilation of openings of films and tv shows that I used to watch growing up. These intros are the roots for my affection of nostalgic imagery.

Dino Game Demo

Created as a reimagination of the Chrome Dinosaur Game. I made a self-playing video game complete with a new start screen, sounds, and art assets.

Kirby Collaboration

This project is a collaborative video intended to showcase our different art styles. We chose to create a video where we each contribute 10 seconds of animation in styles such as pixel, 3D, digital drawing, and claymation.
