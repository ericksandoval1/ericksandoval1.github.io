My Golden Record, 2024

Audacity and Premiere Pro

Description: I edited together several video and audio clips of what I would like to have as my version of the Golden Record currently aboard the Voyager spacecrafts. Some of my favorite sounds growing up were the iconic intro sounds that came before television shows and films. I compiled the audio together in Audacity and later put together a video to accompany it edited in Premiere Pro.
